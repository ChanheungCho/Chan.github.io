About

I am a PhD Candidate in the department of economics at North Carolina State University. My research focuses on Environmental and Resource Economics, Bioeconomics, and Dynamic Decision Modeling, particularly focusing on the dynamic management of resources under structural and observational uncertainty.

In 2024 fall, I will be joining the Science and Technologies for Phosphorus Sustainability (STEPS) Center as a Postdoctoral Research Scholar and will be on the job market during the 2024-2025 academic year.
